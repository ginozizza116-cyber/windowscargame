## Games Overview
The Windows Car Game and ChromeOS Car Game offer exciting and engaging gameplay experiences that challenge players to navigate through various obstacles. Players can choose different vehicles to drive, enhancing their overall gaming experience.

## Quick Start Instructions
1. Download the game from the official website or your respective app store.
2. Install the game following the on-screen instructions.
3. Launch the game and select your preferred mode (Single Player or Multiplayer).
4. Choose your vehicle and track.
5. Use the controls as outlined below to start racing!

## Windows Car Game
### Controls and Tips
- **Arrow Keys:** Move your car left, right, and control speed.
- **Space Bar:** Use nitro boost.
- **P:** Pause the game.
- **Tips:**
  - Always look for shortcuts on each track to gain an advantage over your opponents.
  - Use nitro boosts strategically during straight paths for maximum speed.

## ChromeOS Car Game
### Keyboard Controls
- **A/D or Left/Right Arrow Keys:** Steer left/right.
- **W/S or Up/Down Arrow Keys:** Accelerate/Decelerate.
- **Space Bar:** Activate power-ups.

### Touch Controls
- **On-screen Joystick:** Steer the vehicle.
- **Buttons for Acceleration/Decceleration:** Tap to control speed.
- **Power-up buttons:** Tap to use your items.

## Scoring Systems
Players earn points based on the distance traveled, obstacles avoided, and checkpoints reached. Bonus points are awarded for performing stunts or collecting special items scattered throughout the tracks.

## Browser Compatibility
The games are compatible with the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari. Ensure your browser is updated for the best performance.
